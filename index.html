Data Entry Instructions:

Members Data:

  • Each member's data should be entered on a separate line.
  • Use the format: <Member Name> <Term Year>, separated by a tab.
  • Term representation should be in the format "WS" or "SS" followed by the year (e.g., WS 2023, SS 2021).
  • Example:
    •                     Member 1    WS 2023
                    Member 2    SS 2021

Songs Data:

  • Each song's data should be entered on a separate line.
  • Use the format: <Song Name> <First Performed Year> <Last Performed Year>, separated by a tab.
  • Term representation should be in the format "WS" or "SS" followed by the year (e.g., WS 2023, SS 2021).
  • Example:
    •                     Song 1    WS 2019    WS 2023
                    Song 2    SS 2021    SS 2021

Paste Data:

  • Once your data is formatted correctly, paste it into the textarea below.
  • Ensure the data is separated by tabs (the default delimiter).
  • Click the "Process Data" button to calculate familiarity percentages.
